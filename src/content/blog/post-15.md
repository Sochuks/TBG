Discover these hidden gems in Abuja:

1. **Jabi Lake**: Perfect for picnics and boat rides.
2. **Millennium Park**: A serene escape in the city.
3. **Thought Pyramid Art Center**: A hub for art lovers.
4. **Zuma Rock**: A breathtaking natural landmark.
5. **Gurara Falls**: A stunning waterfall just outside the city.
